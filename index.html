Costs & Pricing

How much does an emergency plumber cost in Durham Region?

Emergency plumbing rates typically range from $150–$350 for a standard call-out in Durham Region. The total cost depends on the time of day, complexity of the issue, and parts required. Our plumbers always provide a quote before starting any work, so there are no surprises.

Is there a call-out fee?

No. The plumbers in our network do not charge call-out fees. You'll receive a free quote and only pay if you choose to go ahead with the work.

Do I pay upfront?

You'll receive a clear quote before any work begins. Payment is typically collected upon completion. Most plumbers accept cash, e-transfer, and major credit cards.

Emergencies

What counts as a plumbing emergency?

A plumbing emergency includes any situation that requires immediate attention to prevent damage or health risks. Common examples include burst pipes, major water leaks, sewage backups, complete loss of water, no hot water in winter, and gas-related plumbing issues.

What should I do while waiting for the plumber?

If possible, turn off the water supply at the main shut-off valve (usually located near your water meter). Place towels or buckets to contain any leaks, and move valuables away from the affected area. If you smell gas, leave the home and call 911.

How fast can a plumber arrive?

For emergencies, our network plumbers typically arrive within 30–60 minutes anywhere in Durham Region. For non-urgent jobs, we'll schedule a convenient time that works for you.
